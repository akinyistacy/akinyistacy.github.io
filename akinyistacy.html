Data terminal equipment

Data terminal equipment(DTE) are devices used to process, host and transmit data on a network.Examples are computers, mobile phones and tables.

Transmission media

Transmission media are physical or wireless channels used to trnsmit data and information from one point to another. Examples of transmission media include copper cables,radio waves,microwaves and satellites.
